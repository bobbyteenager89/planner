## Horseback confirmation — how to see who's actually going

Sharon's note that some people may want to skip Day 2 horseback means we need a definitive headcount before booking. Three options, in order from "no build" to "real feature":

**Option A — Use the existing per-block "Skip" feedback (no build, fastest)**
Each guest already has a "Suggest" button on every block on the share page; one of the options is "Skip." Send a one-line nudge to the 7 adults: *"Open the itinerary, hit Suggest → Skip on the Day 2 horseback block if you'd rather sit it out."* Host review page already shows pending feedback in the inbox. **Trade-off:** silent participants are ambiguous (didn't see it, vs. yes by default).

**Option B — Quick out-of-band poll (no build, definitive)**
Group text or short email to the 7 adults: *"Are you in for horseback on Sunday Jul 19, 11 AM? Reply Y / N / Maybe."* Tally manually, write the headcount into the block's `adult_count`. **Trade-off:** lives outside the app — Andrew has to track replies in his head or in a notes app.

**Option C — Per-block per-person RSVP (real feature, ~half-day build)** *(already on the roadmap)*
Add a yes/no/maybe toggle next to each activity on the personal `/share/my-plan` page. Aggregate to a host headcount panel on the review page. **Trade-off:** real build, but solves this category of question for *every* future activity (rodeo, gondola, kayaking, alpaca farm, golf), not just horseback.

**Recommendation:** **B now, C next.** Text the 7 adults today for horseback (definitive answer in 24h). In parallel, scope C — once it ships, every borderline activity gets clean RSVPs and the booking checklist auto-updates headcounts.
